Refund Policy

REFUND/CANCELLATIONNO REFUND POLICY FOR REGISTRATIONS

We are providing much more content in our courses that’s why we are not providing any refund.

Special circumstances include –

  • In case if the course access is not provided.
  • In case, duplicate payment has been made by the customer. In such a case, the refund will be processed via the same payment channel as the original method of payment.
  • If there is any issue regarding the video quality, which renders the course non-functional.
  • In case of cancellation of any events.

CANCELLATION/POSTPONEMENT/DISCOUNTED ENROLLMENT POLICY

Discounts associated with the original purchase are non-transferrable. Discounted enrollments are only directly transferrable to target seminars currently priced at or above the same discount as the original purchase. Targeted seminars with a lower current discount than the original purchase will need to register at the target seminar, with a discount equivalent to the difference between the original discount, and the current available discount. The administrative fee will also be assessed for such transfers. Discount prices shown online are not valid in conjunction with any other offer and are subject to tax, as applicable.
